Greetings IRIS User!

Before every production release, the IRIS Team sends the following emails to highlight important features that will be included in that release.
Staging Announcment-This email is sent a week after new features have been uploaded to the staging environment.
Pre-Release Announcement-This email is sent the Thursday before the new features get uploaded to production.
Production Release Notes-This email is sent on the day the new features get uploaded to production.
Signup below if you you would like to receive these emails.
